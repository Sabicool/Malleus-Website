Register as a Member

Join Malleus as a general member to receive project updates, gain voting rights at AGMs, and be eligible to nominate for Executive and General Committee positions.

What happens after you register? Within 14 days you'll receive a your.name@malleus.org.au email address, granting you voting rights at AGMs and access to fortnightly General Committee meetings. There are no formal responsibilities — just community membership. You can opt out at any time by emailing admin@malleus.org.au.

Links your membership to your deck subscription on AnkiHub.

Your data is stored securely and used only for Malleus community purposes.
